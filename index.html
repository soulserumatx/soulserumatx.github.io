Soul Serum ATX is a female-fronted original hard rock band forged in the live music capital of the world — Austin, Texas. We write, record, and perform music that hits hard, cuts deep, and leaves you wanting more.

With powerhouse vocals at the front and a tight, battle-tested lineup behind her, Soul Serum ATX brings an intensity to every stage that you can feel in your chest. Our sound is rooted in hard rock — heavy riffs, driving rhythms, and melodies that stay with you long after the last note fades.

We're not playing covers — every song is an original, born from real experiences and real emotion. Austin is our home, and we're here to make noise.
